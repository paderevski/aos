\section{Vector functions Quiz 1}\label{vector-functions-quiz-1}}

The position of a particle at any time \(t \geq 0\) is given by
\(x(t) = t^2 - 2, y(t) = \frac23 t^3\)

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\def\itemsep{2in}
\item
  Find the magnitude of the velocity vector at \(t=2\).
\item
  Set up but do not evaluate an integral expression to calculate the
  total distance traveled by the particle from \(t=0\) to \(t=4\).
\item
  Find \(\dfrac{dy}{dx}\) as a function of \(t\) and also as a function
  of \(x\).
\item
  At what time \(t\) is the particle on the \(y\)-axis? What is its
  acceleration vector at that time?
\end{enumerate}
